ABOUT NSSC

The NSSC (Nagpur Subsection Congress) is being hosted for the Second time by the established Nagpur Subsection.
The general notion of a Congress is to bring in potential speakers and listeners from all over the country to make the most out of an exhilarating 3 day event. From gaining an outlook on the latest technological advances to solving complex social issues and networking with the broadest array of enthusiastic young minds, we have everything you want.
These action packed three day schedule will include some promising speakers delivering various diversified talks, interactive sessions, workshops and all about the glorious culture of Nagpur!
